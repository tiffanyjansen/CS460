Fun Facts about Cats:

  • When a domestic cat goes after mice, about 1 pounce in 3 results in a catch.
  • Domestic house cats are actually crepuscular, meaning they are most active at dusk and dawn.
  • Cats have 4 four different meanings for "'meow'".
  • It is well-known that cats sleep from 14 to 18 hours a day.
  • Their taste buds instead respond to amino acids, bitter tastes, and acids.
